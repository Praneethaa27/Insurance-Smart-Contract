# Sample Interactions

1. Issue Policy (by Insurer)
   - Input: user address, 1 ETH premium, 5 ETH coverage, 3 months, 0 years

2. Pay Premium (by Policyholder)
   - Input: policyId = 1
   - Value: 1 ETH

3. Submit Claim (by Policyholder)
   - Input: policyId = 1, amount = 1 ETH, reason = "accident"

4. Approve Claim (by Insurer)
   - Input: claimId = 1

5. Pay Claim (by Insurer)
   - Input: claimId = 1
